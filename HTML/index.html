Two Dice is all you really Need

When you play craps you are really just wasting time, but it can be a fun way to waste time. The roar of a hot table is attractive, dismay and disbelieve of a cold table is just depressing, but sadness makes you appreciate the joy. Craps, crabs, throwing bones, or whatever is just a game that is played by many because it is enjoyable.

I like the game so I play it. When it is no longer fun I will no longer play it.
